Organic Pest Management

A pesticide is a substance that is intended for preventing, destroying, repelling, or mitigating any pest.
Pesticides include insecticides, fungicides, disinfectants, repellents, and biopesticides.
Organic pesticides are derived from natural sources, such as plants, minerals, or animals.
It is important to note that the results may not be immediate, as natural farming practices often require more time to show their full benefits.
